Our Products

Our flagship product is an open source, cross-platform coffee roasting application. Currently, our application is compatible with the FreshRoast SR700 coffee roaster, with the potential to support any computer assisted, home roasting device. In order to share recipes, we have created the Roastero Cloud Platform designed as a central hub for users to share and explore roast recipes.

Our Philosophy

We decided at an early stage that we wanted our core application to be free and open source for the life of the product. The current state of home roasting involves a lot of modification, tinkering, and hacking. Released under GPLv3, Roastero continues this legacy, allowing users to have full control over their roasters. While designing the application, we wanted to maintain this level of control while providing a simplistic interface for an inexperienced user.

When Can I Download Roastero?

Roastero is currently being meticulously tested by a small group of beta users to ensure its quality upon release. Our official release to all users will be available in July. We will then begin work on the Roastero cloud platform and provide official release dates for the platform at a later date.
