Roastero Features

Roastero significantly extends the capabilities of the
FreshRoast SR700 while providing an open framework for other coffee roasters.

  • Clean, Intuitive Design

    Roastero includes live graph plotting in addition to a full set of gauges to provide critical roast information. With all of the roast data live, at users’ fingertips, users don’t have to rely only on their senses while roasting coffee.

  • Powerful Roaster Controls

    Control your roaster by setting a temperature value rather than heat settings with the aid of a software based PID controller. Quickly adjust temperature, time, and fan speed on the fly to achieve the perfect roast.

  • Roast Repeatability

    Once a perfect roast is created with our built in recipe system, it can be roasted exactly the same, every time.

  • Social Recipe Sharing

    With our Roastero Cloud Platform, users can easily share and download roast recipes with other roaster users allowing community collaboration and a low barrier of entry for a novice roaster.

  • Precise Roast Logging

    Never miss a degree or second with Roastero’s powerful logging features. Roastero will automatically create a log of each recipe, allowing a user add cupping notes and repeat the roast at a later date.

  • Active Community

    With an active community to share/rate recipes, provide recipe feedback, and ask questions, Roastero provides the complete package for both novice and experienced home roasters alike.
